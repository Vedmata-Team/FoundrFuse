Startups Don't Need More Noise. They Need the Right Signal.

The Problem

Traditional networking platforms are outdated, bloated, and biased towards already-funded players. Cold emails go unanswered, networking events are time-consuming, and many talented founders outside major hubs struggle to get noticed.

  • Endless cold emails with low response rates
  • Network bias favoring established connections
  • Geographic disadvantages for non-hub founders
  • Time-consuming fundraising process

Our Solution

FoundrFuse levels the field with mutual swipe-based matching, AI-powered discovery, and transparent pitch verification — helping startups in Tier-2/3 cities get seen and investors discover hidden gems before anyone else.

  • Intuitive, mobile-first swipe interface
  • AI-powered matching based on compatibility
  • Equal visibility regardless of location
  • Verified profiles for trust and transparency

Our Journey So Far

From idea to impact

2023 Q1

The Idea Takes Shape

After struggling to connect with investors for his own startup, Parth conceptualizes FoundrFuse as a mobile-first matching platform for founders and investors.

2023 Q2

First MVP Launch

The first version of FoundrFuse launches with basic profile creation and swipe functionality. 100 beta users test the platform and provide valuable feedback.

2023 Q3

Seed Funding & Expansion

FoundrFuse secures seed funding and expands the team. Enhanced matching algorithms and verification systems are implemented.

2023 Q4

1,000 Users Milestone

Platform reaches 1,000 active users with a 30% match rate. First batch of successful funding stories emerge from early matches.

2024 Q1

Premium Features Launch

Introduction of premium subscription model with advanced filters, unlimited swipes, and analytics dashboard. AI matching algorithm improves compatibility.

Present

Growing Community

FoundrFuse continues to grow with thousands of active users across multiple countries. The platform has facilitated over $50 million in funding deals.
