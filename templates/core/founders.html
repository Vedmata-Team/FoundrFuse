Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions from founders

FoundrFuse welcomes founders from all industries and at all stages - from pre-seed to Series B. Whether you're a tech startup, a D2C brand, or a social enterprise, our platform helps connect you with investors interested in your specific sector and stage.

Complete your profile thoroughly with clear information about your business, traction metrics, team background, and funding needs. Upload a concise pitch deck highlighting your unique value proposition. Our AI system will provide feedback to help optimize your profile. Premium users also benefit from profile boosts and advanced matching algorithms.

Your profile should include: business overview, traction metrics (users, revenue, growth), team background and expertise, funding history (if any), amount you're raising, intended use of funds, market size and opportunity, and competitive advantage. Premium users can also showcase customer testimonials and media mentions.

Timing varies based on your industry, stage, and funding needs. Many founders receive their first matches within 1-2 weeks of creating their profile. Premium users typically see faster results due to increased visibility and unlimited swipes. Remember that quality matches matter more than quantity - our system prioritizes relevant connections over random introductions.
